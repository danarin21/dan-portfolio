Integer Intel

When we're not dusting aliens from the outer rings of the solar system, Adam Kendall and I are busy drawing up new business opportunities in our fields of expertise. We've been gaming since Mario was 8-bit, and saw an industry opportunity for our retail agency to pursue.

While our leadership identified primary targets amongst leading publishers, our mission was to establish Integer as the HQ of retail intelligence.

Retail HQ

Inspired by our favorite first-person shooters, we designed Integer Intel to capture the insights and strategy behind our agency while carving lines into new territory.

Mission Intel

We built the HQ site around the idea of gathering clandestine intelligence for the looming battle of retail. Each target was given an access key that unlocked some of our proprietary intel.

Battle for Retail

Video game success is largely determined by pre-orders and launch date sales. Building our strategy around these barriers while also exploring engagement with the audience post-launch, would help our clients conquer new retail territory.
