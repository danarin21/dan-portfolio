AArete Careers

Website build for the careers section of AArete. I lead UX and visual design for this page of the site overhaul which included a new culture section and specific career tracks.

View site: https://www.aarete.com/careers

Culture

AArete has a number of community networks in which employees enjoy participating. Showcasing the company culture at its many functions was a new endeavor as it was previously self-contained in social media.


Culture sections can be easily updated with new programs or content via custom gallery layouts.

Benefits & Perks

A simple and interactive showcase of AArete's employee offerings in a responsive grid. Sub-navigation links accelerate user experience across the page.

Career Tracks

Prospective employees want to envision a career at AArete, so we created a tool that breaks down department career paths. Here, candidates can read up on each position's role and responsibilities while gauging what is expected to earn a promotion.
