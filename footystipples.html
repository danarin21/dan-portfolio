Footy Stipples

To challenge myself in a new craft, I started a lettering project with a twist. Stippling requires an incredible amount of patience by bringing a work to life via millions of tiny dots. Each work brings to life elements of the club's history and symbology.

Arsenal

I chose to illustrate a letterform from each English top-flight club, beginning with my beloved Arsenal. Arsenal Football Club has won the FA Cup a record 13 times in its history, including three of the last four competitions. The FA Cup is England's prestigious domestic tournament in which clubs from the top four flights compete for the title each year.
