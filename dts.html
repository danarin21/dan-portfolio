Disney Theatrical Sales

Website build for the group sales division of Disney Theatrical Group. I handled UX/UI design and lead the agency switch to prototyping with Adobe XD.

View site: http://disneytheatricalsales.com/

Disney Shows

Each Disney Theatrical show: The Lion King, Aladdin, and Frozen were given portals to book tickets to a performance for large groups. The input form and calendar module are synced together to allow the user to choose future dates and auto-fill simultaneously.

Background image transitions between branding and photography from the show.

Theatre Tour

Visitors also have the ability to book a theatre tour and catch a glimpse of Disney magic behind-the-scenes.

Mobile Considerations

While not the primary form of booking, mobile proved to be a valuable gateway for accessing contact information of sales representatives. Our primary goal here was to present all the necessary information in a clean and organized manner to aid decision-making.
