The Lion King

As digital agency of record for Disney's The Lion King musical, we worked on a number of content pieces for the show's marketing, including: digital billboards, website, ad units, and social assets. We handled both the Broadway production and national tour of the show.

Larger Than Life

In the heart of Times Square, The iconic ABC SuperSign is one of the largest and most dynamic digital billboards in the city. During the summer of 2016, the sign was upgraded to process HD video and live feeds. Our team re-formatted The Lion King's segment to fit across varying layouts as different sections were blacked out during construction.
