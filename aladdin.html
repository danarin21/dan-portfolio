Aladdin

The challenge with working on Disney, is finding ways to balance the brand's magic with the proper sales messaging. For Aladdin the Musical, I helped translate their new photography-based branding to the web. This approach targeted younger couples and families, creating a more dramatic look for the show.

I also lead the brand's art direction across various media channels including outdoor video billboards and display ads.

View site: http://www.aladdinthemusical.com/

A Whole New Look

With The Lion King firmly rooted on Broadway, Aladdin faced the challenge of appealing to a new demographic: young adults. The photography and messaging conveys mystery with hints of sex appeal. We let the imagery do the heavy lifting by splashing it across the home page as the dominating element.

Mobile Considerations

We granted the character photography all of the vertical real estate it deserved. Secondarily, quick links to ticket purchasing are omni-present.

Times Square Billboard

The ABC SuperSign is one of the city's most iconic billboards reaching millions of people every day. After it was upgraded to a high-definition LED display, I lead the art direction of the show's art and messaging across the entire sign.
